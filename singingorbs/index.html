Singing Orbs

Meditative Sound Spheres.

About

Transform your screen into a tranquil sound garden. Trace your finger through glowing orbs and create beautiful, harmonious tones that resonate and fade like singing bowls. Perfect for meditation, focus, stress relief, or winding down.

How to Use

  • Touch and glide through orbs to create sounds
  • Each orb produces a pure, soothing tone tuned to musical scales designed to calm the mind
  • Tones sustain and fade naturally like singing bowls
  • No musical experience needed—every combination sounds beautiful

Premium Features

  • All 12 musical keys — Find the perfect tone for your mood
  • 9 unique scales — From dreamy Whole Tone to serene Japanese (In) scale
  • Custom backgrounds — Transform the orbs with your own photos
